Imran Kibria

Ph.D. Researcher, Computer Science & Engineering

I work on developing and applying signal processing, optimization, and deep learning techniques for advanced reconstruction of cardiac magnetic resonance imaging (MRI). My doctoral research, funded by NIH Grant R01HL151697, focuses on motion-robust, real-time whole-heart flow imaging innovations. I am co-advised by Rizwan Ahmad and Lee Potter at The Ohio State University. In summer 2025, I completed a research internship with the MR Feature Development team at Canon Medical Research USA (CMRU). I also received the Graduate Associate Leadership Award (2024).
